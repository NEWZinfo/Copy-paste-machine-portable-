
# ⚡ Portable Copy-Paste Machine
### *Turning Physical Writing into Real-World Copy-Paste*
---
Demo video [Watch here ]

## 🎯 The Vision
> What if you could copy a notebook page… and paste it into another—instantly?

We are building a **real-world copy-paste machine** that captures handwritten or printed content and reproduces it automatically—no manual rewriting needed.

---

## 🧠 The Problem
📚 Students spend hours copying notes  
🚫 Phones are restricted in classrooms  
📉 Time is wasted on writing instead of learning  

---

## 💡 Our Solution
A **custom-built machine** that:
- 📸 Captures content from a page  
- ⚙️ Processes it using microcontrollers  
- 🖨️ Recreates it on another page
